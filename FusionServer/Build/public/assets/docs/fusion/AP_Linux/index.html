Connect to MyBot via Linux

Steps to connect with Linux

1. Turn on your computer's Wi-Fi capabilities. Click on the Wi-Fi symbol in the toolbar in the upper right hand corner.

2. Find the MyBot Access Point SSID and select it to enter the passkey (i.e. RecruitAP_99be01).

3. Enter the passkey for your MyBot. Then click "OK" to connect.
Default Passkey: mrirecruit

4. Your MyBot is now connected to your Linux computer.

Questions?

Contact Boxlight Robotics at support@BoxlightRobotics.com with a detailed description of the steps you have taken and observations you have made.
