Charging the Fusion battery

How to charge the battery

To charge the Fusion battery pack, first make sure the battery is connected.

Then connect the accompanying battery charger to the CHRG port on the Fusion. The Fusion must be off in order to charge the battery. If the Fusion is on while the battery and charger are connected, the battery will not be charged.

The charger must be plugged in to the CHRG port as shown, with the BLACK wire to the right in the battery will not be charged.

When the charger is connected and charging the battery, the light on the charger will turn red. Once the battery is fully charged the light will turn green. The charger may get warm when charging a battery..
