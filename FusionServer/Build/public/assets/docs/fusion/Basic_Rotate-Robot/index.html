Basic Rotate Robot Control

The Rotate toolbox contains blocks to rotate right and left at a variety of angles.

List of available blocks:

  • Rotate Right 45°
  • Rotate Right 90°
  • Rotate Right 180°
  • Rotate Left 45°
  • Rotate Left 90°
  • Rotate Left 180°

Rotate Right 45°

Rotates the robot to the right (clockwise) at an angle of 45°.
